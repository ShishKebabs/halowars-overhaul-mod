1. extract this zip to your c drive C:\


2. put the "startup" folder in to the "halo wars" directory e.g. mine is:
	F:\SteamGames\steamapps\common\HaloWarsDE


3. put the "ModManifest.txt" file inside:
	C:\Users\REPLACE_THIS_WITH_YOUR_ACCOUNT\AppData\Local\Halo Wars
